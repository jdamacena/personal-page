This app was very fun to build: I was playing with my Arduino, doing some things that I wanted to control with my phone via Bluetooth. But then I get tired of having to deal with the bad interface that I had on the app that I was using, so I decided to build my own. It allows you to send commands to an Arduino that have a Bluetooth module. In the app you can create a customizable interface according to the project you have: you can change the controls (e.g. buttons) and the commands that are sent, you can even speak to the app and control your Arduino using your voice! Pretty cool, huh?

Made with:

  • Java;
  • Kotlin;
  • Android Studio.

Featuring:

  • C/C++ (for Arduino) & Arduino IDE.
